Features

Everything you need from a
meeting intelligence app.

From recording to action items MeetMind handles it all with AI, so you can focus on the conversation.

One-Tap Recording

Start recording with a single tap. Echo cancellation and background recording keep your audio clean, even near a laptop speaker.

Golden Template Notes

AI generates structured notes with TL;DR, key decisions, action items table, discussion points, and open questions.

Smart Chat

Ask "How many meetings this week?" or "What action items are on me?" and get instant, formatted answers.

Voice Todos

Say "remind me to send the proposal by Friday" and MeetMind extracts the task, date, and priority automatically.

Communication Coach

Get feedback on filler words, speaking pace, talk ratio, and questions asked. Improve with every meeting.

Live Transcription

See real-time words on screen while recording. Never miss what was said — even if you zone out for a moment.

Key Moments

Auto-detects decisions, action items, and questions with tappable timestamps. Jump to any key moment instantly.

Calendar Integration

Syncs with Google Calendar and Outlook. See today's meetings and start recording with context from your last call.

Streaks & Stats

Track your meeting consistency, productivity scores, and level up from Beginner to Meeting Master.

How It Works

Three steps to meeting clarity.

No setup, no bots, no plugins. Just tap record and let AI do the rest.

1

Record

Tap the mic button to start recording. Works with laptop speakers, AirPods, or the phone on your desk. Background recording stays on when you minimize.

→
2

AI Processes

Groq Whisper transcribes your audio in seconds. Then Llama 3.3 70B generates structured notes with the Golden Template format TL;DR, decisions, action items.

→
3

Get Notes

Receive a professional meeting brief with key decisions, action items with owners and deadlines, open questions, and AI coaching insights. Share or export instantly.
