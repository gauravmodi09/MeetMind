Tired of juggling a recorder, a note-taking app, a to-do list, and a shared doc just to survive one meeting? MeetMind replaces all of them with a single tap.

**The problem is real.** After every meeting you scramble to remember what was said, who promised what, and what happens next. Important decisions slip through the cracks. Action items vanish. You end up in another meeting to recap the last one.

**MeetMind fixes this.**

Tap Record. Talk. When you're done, MeetMind's AI reads the full transcript and generates a structured brief — summary, key decisions, action items, and follow-ups — in seconds. Copy it, share it, or let it live in the perfectly organized client folder where it belongs.

**How it works:**
1. Record — One tap starts a high-quality local recording. No bots join your call. No awkward "this meeting is being recorded by an AI" moments. Just you and your microphone.
2. AI processes — Groq-powered transcription runs at 216x real-time speed. Then AI structures everything into a clean, readable brief with sections you actually care about.
3. Share — Copy your brief with one tap. Paste it into Slack, email, Notion, or anywhere. Your teammates get a polished summary, not a wall of raw transcript.

**Key features:**

- One-tap recording — Start recording any meeting (in-person, phone, video call) instantly. Works in the background.
- AI-powered briefs — Get a structured summary with decisions, action items, and follow-ups. Not a transcript dump — a real brief.

- Voice todos — Say "Call Sarah about the proposal by Friday" and MeetMind creates a todo with the right due date. No typing required.
- Apple Watch — Start and stop recordings from your wrist. Perfect for in-person meetings where pulling out your phone feels awkward.
- Widgets — See upcoming todos and recent meetings right from your home screen.

**Built different:**

- No subscription required — MeetMind is free. The AI runs on Groq's generous free tier. No trials, no paywalls, no "upgrade to unlock AI."
- Privacy-first — Your recordings stay on your device. Audio is sent to Groq only for transcription and is never stored on their servers. We don't track you, we don't sell your data, we don't show ads.
- No meeting bots — Unlike Otter, Fireflies, or Granola, MeetMind never joins your call as a participant. Record locally and discreetly.
- Instant sharing — Your brief is formatted and ready to paste anywhere. No reformatting, no cleanup.

**Who it's for:**

# MeetMind Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 18, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

MeetMind ("the App") is developed by an independent developer committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

## 1. Microphone Usage

MeetMind requests access to your device's microphone for two purposes:

- **Meeting recording:** When you tap the Record button, the App captures audio through your device's microphone. This audio is stored locally on your device and is used solely to generate transcriptions and AI-powered meeting briefs.
- **Voice todos:** When you use the voice input feature to create todos, the App briefly accesses the microphone to capture your spoken instruction. This audio is processed to extract the todo text and any date references, then discarded.

The App never records audio in the background without your knowledge. Recording is always initiated explicitly by you and indicated by visible on-screen and system-level indicators.

## 2. Data Storage

- **Local storage:** All meeting recordings, transcripts, briefs, todos, and client folder data are stored locally on your device. This data remains under your full control.
- **iCloud sync (optional):** If you enable iCloud sync in Settings, your meeting data (recordings, transcripts, briefs, todos, and folder structures) is synced across your Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. This data is governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy and is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. MeetMind has no access to your iCloud account or credentials.

## 3. Third-Party API Usage (Groq Transcription)

When you record a meeting, the audio file is sent to Groq's API for transcription. Here is what you need to know:

- **What is sent:** The audio file from your recording.
- **What is returned:** A text transcription of the audio.
- **Data retention by Groq:** Groq does not store your audio files or transcription results after processing is complete. Audio is processed in memory and discarded. Groq's privacy policy is available at https://groq.com/privacy-policy/.
- **No secondary use:** Your audio is not used by Groq to train models, improve services, or for any purpose other than generating the transcription you requested.

No other third-party services receive your data.

## 4. No Data Selling

MeetMind does not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal data or meeting content to any third party. Period.

## 5. No Tracking or Analytics

MeetMind does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or fingerprinting mechanisms. The App does not track your behavior, build a profile, or share usage data with anyone.

## 6. No Advertising

MeetMind does not display advertisements of any kind. There are no ad networks integrated into the App.

## 7. Data Deletion

MeetMind does not maintain any server-side accounts or cloud storage beyond your personal iCloud (if enabled).

- **Delete the App = delete all local data.** Uninstalling MeetMind from your device permanently removes all locally stored recordings, transcripts, briefs, todos, and settings.
- **iCloud data:** If you used iCloud sync, you can delete synced data through your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage. Disabling iCloud sync within the App stops future syncing but does not delete previously synced data from iCloud — you must do that through Apple's iCloud settings.
- **No server-side data to delete:** Since MeetMind does not maintain user accounts or server-side storage, there is no additional data to request deletion of.

## 8. Children's Privacy

MeetMind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at the Privacy Policy URL listed in the App Store and within the App's settings screen. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect the revision date.
